#### Scope

- Source
  - Digital text
  - __Image text__
- Writing type
  - __Printed words__
  - Handwritten words
- Length of text block
  - Words
  - __Sentences/lines__
  - __Paragraphs__
- Hierarchical levels
  - Script/language category
    - Shape of individual characters
  - Specific language
    - Language profile
      - Characters/n-gram distribution & sequences of characters/n-gram distribution
